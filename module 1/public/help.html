Community Event Portal Help

How do I register for an event?
Navigate to the Events section and fill out the registration form with your details. Select the event you want to attend and click the Register button.
Is there a cost to attend events?
Some events have fees while others are free. The fee (if any) will be displayed when you select an event type in the registration form.
How can I find events near me?
Click the "Find Nearby Events" button in the Events section to see events based on your current location. You'll need to allow location access in your browser.
Can I cancel my registration?
Yes, please contact us at events@community.org with your registration details to request cancellation.
How do I save my event preferences?
Your preferred event type is automatically saved when you select it in the form. You can clear your preferences using the "Clear Preferences" button.
